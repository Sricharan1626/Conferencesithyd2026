About Conference 2026

International Conference on Deep Learning Innovations for Smart Humanized AI

The Department of IoT and Intelligent Systems, Symbiosis Institiute of technology Hyderabad, is organizing the International Conference on Deep Learning Innovations for Smart Humanized AI (Conference 2026) from 17 – 19 December 2026 in hybrid mode at Symbiosis Institute of Technology Hyderabad and online.

Conference Theme

"Generative Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning and Internet of Things"

The theme of Conference 2026 emphasizes the convergence of Generative Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to design next-generation intelligent systems that are adaptive, ethical, and human-centric. Generative AI and Deep Learning are revolutionizing domains such as healthcare, autonomous systems, natural language processing, and smart content creation, while IoT provides seamless connectivity and real-time data integration. Together, these technologies are paving the way for innovations in smart cities, communication networks, robotics, and sustainable resource management. By fostering interdisciplinary research and industry collaboration, the theme envisions intelligent ecosystems that enhance quality of life, ensure transparency and inclusivity, and drive societal progress responsibly.
